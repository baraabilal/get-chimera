What is Actually Chimera?

Large Language Model LLM

At its essence, Chimera is a Large Language Model (LLM) tailored specifically for your business. Unlike generic AI, Chimera is built upon and evolves with your unique data ecosystem. This means every piece of information—every email, transaction, customer interaction—feeds into Chimera, making it smarter and more attuned to your business needs over time.
